Resources and Transformation in Pre-Modern Societies

CONFERENCE POSTPONED: we regert to annouce that the ReSoc conference, originally taking place on 14-15 May 2020, is postponed to Novermber 2020 due to the outbreak of COVID-19 in Europe. We will give further notice on the specific dates. Meanwhile, please check back for updates on the conference.
The ReSoc conference Resources and Transformation in pre-modern Societies is organised by the Leibniz Post-Doc School “Resources in Society” (ReSoc) hosted by the Ruhr-University Bochum (RUB) and the Deutsches Bergbau-Museum Bochum (DBM). The aim of the conference is to provide a forum for a transdisciplinary discourse on transformation of knowledge, culture and landscapes in relation to resources. In three sessions, with two distinguished keynote speakers each, we will cover aspects with a multitude of different methodological and theoretical perspectives.
